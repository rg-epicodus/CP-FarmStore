Some of our work

Aimee's Garden designs each urban farm to spec, with options ranging from raised beds to rooftop farms.

Features and services

From backyard raised beds to rooftop farms on grocery stores, we’ve installed and continue to maintain urban farms of all shapes and sizes around the Portland area.

  • Consultation

    Our skilled team of horticulture and garden education experts provide the expertise to grow the highest yield possible for you, or teach you how to successfully grow for yourself.

  • Education

    Aimee's Garden staff are experienced and passionate organic farmers specialized in designing and maintaining urban farms for high production value and educational purposes.

  • Maintenance

    Full maintenance services are available to clients looking to maximize yield in whatever space is available at their home or business, whether on rooftops, ground level, or using indoor growing systems.
